Principles of Agile

Agile methodologies are based on the following principles:

  • Customer satisfaction through early and continuous software delivery.
  • Embrace changing requirements, even late in development.
  • Deliver working software frequently.
  • Collaboration between business stakeholders and developers.
  • Motivated individuals should be trusted to get the job done.
  • Face-to-face conversation is the most efficient and effective method of conveying information.
  • Working software is the primary measure of progress.
  • Sustainable development, able to maintain a constant pace indefinitely.
  • Continuous attention to technical excellence and good design.
  • Simplicity is essential.
  • The best architectures, requirements, and designs emerge from self-organizing teams.
  • At regular intervals, the team reflects on how to become more effective.

Benefits of Agile

Implementing Agile methodologies can lead to numerous benefits, including:

  • Increased flexibility and adaptability to change.
  • Improved product quality.
  • Higher customer satisfaction.
  • Enhanced team collaboration and communication.
  • Faster delivery of functional software.
